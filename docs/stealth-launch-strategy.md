## Executive Summary

BooGMe is positioned to launch as a premium chess coaching marketplace that solves a critical problem in the chess education space: the lack of trust and payment protection in coach-student relationships. This document outlines a coach-first stealth launch strategy designed to build supply-side momentum before opening to students, ensuring a high-quality marketplace from day one.

The strategy focuses on attracting **50 elite coaches** in the first 90 days through targeted outreach, leveraging your existing network and credibility as a grandmaster, Mizzou Chess Program Director, and C-Squared Podcast host. With 50 coaches onboarded, the platform can sustainably serve 500-1,000 students while maintaining quality and personalized matching.

## Phase 2: Coach Recruitment (Weeks 5-12)

### Objective
Onboard **50 elite coaches** through targeted outreach, leveraging existing networks and credibility.

### Target Coach Profile

**Ideal Founding Coaches:**
- FIDE rating: 2200+ (National Master or above)
- Teaching experience: 2+ years
- English proficiency: Conversational or better
- Online presence: Active on social media or streaming platforms
- Geographic diversity: Mix of US, Europe, India, Latin America

**Why 50 Coaches?**
- Sufficient variety for AI matching algorithm
- Manageable for manual quality control
- Creates sense of exclusivity ("Founding Coach" status)
- Sustainable coach-to-student ratio (1:10-20)

### Outreach Strategy

#### 2.1 Tier 1: Personal Network (Target: 10-15 coaches)

**Your Direct Connections:**
- Mizzou Chess Program coaches and alumni
- Grand Chess Tour commentator colleagues
- C-Squared Podcast guests (chess professionals)
- St. Louis Chess Club network

**Approach:**
- Personal email or DM
- Emphasize "founding coach" exclusive status
- Offer early revenue share bonus (first 6 months: 85% instead of 80%)
- Frame as partnership, not vendor relationship

**Email Template:**
```
Subject: Founding Coach Invitation - BooGMe

Hi [Name],

I'm reaching out personally because I'm launching BooGMe, a chess coaching platform built to solve the problems we've all experienced: unreliable payments, platform fees eating into earnings, and lack of trust between coaches and students.

As a fellow coach and GM, I've designed BooGMe with three core principles:
1. Fair compensation (you keep 80-85%)
2. Payment protection (escrow system, no disputes)
3. Quality over quantity (invite-only coaches)

I'd love to have you as one of our founding coaches. This means:
- Early access and input on platform features
- "Founding Coach" badge on your profile
- 85% revenue share for your first 6 months
- No payment details needed until you earn $100

Would you be open to a quick call this week to discuss?

Best,
Cristian
```

#### 2.2 Tier 2: Chess Streaming & Content Creators (Target: 15-20 coaches)

**Platforms to Target:**
- Twitch chess streamers (2K+ followers)
- YouTube chess educators (10K+ subscribers)
- Chess.com streamers and titled players

**Why Streamers?**
- Already have audience (built-in student base)
- Comfortable with online teaching
- Influencer effect (their audience follows to BooGMe)
- Content creation potential (reviews, tutorials featuring BooGMe)

**Outreach Method:**
- Twitter/X DM or email (find via Twitch/YouTube "About" sections)
- Offer affiliate partnership: 10% of first 3 months of students they refer
- Provide unique referral code for tracking

#### 2.3 Tier 3: Chess.com & Lichess Coaches (Target: 15-20 coaches)

**Target Coaches:**
- Chess.com coaches with 50+ reviews and 4.5+ rating
- Lichess coaches with active profiles
- Coaches frustrated with high platform fees (Chess.com takes 30%)

**Value Proposition:**
- Lower fees (15-20% vs. 30%)
- Better payment protection
- More control over pricing and scheduling

**Outreach Method:**
- LinkedIn InMail or Twitter DM
- Reference their Chess.com/Lichess profile to show you've done research
- Emphasize "diversify your income streams" angle

#### 2.4 Tier 4: FIDE Database Outreach (Target: 5-10 coaches)

**Target:**
- Titled players (FM, IM, GM) aged 25-40
- Countries with strong English proficiency (India, Philippines, Eastern Europe)
- Players with stagnant or declining ratings (may be transitioning to coaching)

**Method:**
- Find emails via FIDE profile, personal websites, or LinkedIn
- Cold outreach with personalized message referencing their chess career
- Offer free onboarding consultation

### Coach Onboarding Process

**Step 1: Application Review (Manual)**
- Review FIDE profile, teaching experience, online presence
- Check for red flags (disputes, negative reviews elsewhere)
- Approve or decline within 48 hours

**Step 2: Onboarding Call (15-20 minutes)**
- Explain platform mechanics (escrow, matching, pricing)
- Set expectations (response time, professionalism)
- Gather profile information (bio, specialties, availability)

**Step 3: Profile Creation**
- Coach fills out detailed profile (teaching style, openings, student success stories)
- Upload profile photo and optional intro video
- Set hourly rate ($30-$150/hour recommended range)

**Step 4: Stripe Connect Delayed Onboarding**
- Coach can start teaching immediately
- Stripe payment details required only after earning $100
- Reduces friction in signup process

### Success Metrics

**Week 8 Target:** 25 coaches onboarded  
**Week 12 Target:** 50 coaches onboarded

**Quality Indicators:**
- Average coach rating: 2300+ FIDE
- Geographic diversity: 10+ countries represented
- Specialty coverage: Openings, endgames, tactics, tournament prep, junior coaching

## Phase 3: Soft Launch to Students (Weeks 13-16)

### Objective
Open platform to first 100-200 students through controlled, invite-only access.

### Student Acquisition Strategy

#### 3.1 Waitlist Conversion
- Email waitlist subscribers (collected during stealth phase)
- Offer "Founding Student" perks: 20% off first 5 lessons
- Stagger invites (25 per week) to manage onboarding load

#### 3.2 Coach Referrals
- Each coach gets 5 invite codes for their existing students
- Incentivize with bonus: $25 credit for each student who books 3+ lessons

#### 3.3 C-Squared Podcast Announcement
- Dedicate episode or segment to BooGMe launch
- Offer exclusive promo code for C-Squared audience
- Emphasize your personal involvement and quality standards

#### 3.4 Targeted Ads (Small Budget: $500-$1,000)
- Facebook/Instagram ads targeting chess players aged 18-45
- Geographic focus: US, UK, Canada, Germany
- Ad creative: Testimonials from founding coaches, payment protection messaging

### Student Onboarding Flow

**Step 1: Sign Up & Quiz**
- User creates account (email or social login)
- Takes Coach Finder Quiz (skill level, goals, learning style, budget)

**Step 2: AI Matching**
- Algorithm suggests 3-5 coaches based on quiz responses
- User can browse all coaches or filter by specialty/price

**Step 3: Book Trial Lesson**
- First lesson discounted (50% off) to reduce commitment friction
- Payment held in escrow until lesson completion

**Step 4: Lesson Completion & Review**
- Student confirms lesson completion within 48 hours
- Leaves rating and review
- Payment released to coach (minus platform fee)

### Success Metrics

**Week 16 Target:** 100 students onboarded, 200 lessons booked

## Key Risks & Mitigation

### Risk 1: Slow Coach Adoption
**Mitigation:**
- Offer higher revenue share (85%) for first 6 months
- Provide free marketing support (profile optimization, social media shoutouts)
- Create coach referral program (bonus for bringing other coaches)

### Risk 2: Payment Disputes
**Mitigation:**
- Clear terms of service and lesson policies
- 48-hour confirmation window for students
- Manual review of disputed lessons
- Insurance fund (5% of revenue) for edge cases

### Risk 3: Quality Control Issues
**Mitigation:**
- Maintain invite-only coach onboarding (no open registration)
- Monitor coach ratings and student feedback
- Implement "probation" system for coaches with <4.0 rating
- Remove coaches with repeated complaints

### Risk 4: Competition from Chess.com/Lichess
**Mitigation:**
- Differentiate on payment protection and coach compensation
- Target coaches frustrated with existing platforms
- Build community and loyalty through founding coach program
- Emphasize personal touch (you as founder/GM)

BooGMe's stealth launch strategy prioritizes quality over speed, building a foundation of elite coaches before scaling to students. By leveraging your credibility as a GM, Mizzou Program Director, and C-Squared host, you can attract top-tier coaching talent and establish BooGMe as the premium alternative to existing platforms.
