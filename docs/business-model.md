## 2. Revenue Model

BooGMe employs a **multi-stream revenue model** to ensure sustainable growth and diversified income.

### Primary Revenue Streams

#### 2.1 Platform Commission (Projected: 50-60% of Revenue)

The core revenue driver is a commission on each lesson transaction.

| Commission Tier | Rate | Criteria |
|-----------------|------|----------|
| **Standard** | 18% | Default for all coaches |
| **Verified** | 15% | Coaches with 4.5+ rating, 50+ lessons |
| **Elite** | 12% | GMs/IMs with 100+ lessons, 4.8+ rating |

**Example Transaction:**
- Lesson price: $60
- Standard commission (18%): $10.80
- Coach receives: $49.20
- Platform revenue: $10.80

**Projected Annual Revenue (Year 1):**
- 5,000 active students × 4 lessons/month × $50 avg × 18% = **$1.8M**

#### 2.2 Premium Subscriptions (Projected: 20-25% of Revenue)

**Projected Annual Revenue (Year 1):**

## 3. Payment Protection System

BooGMe's **escrow-based payment system** is a key differentiator that builds trust and reduces friction.

### How It Works

```
Student Books Lesson → Payment Captured (Held) → Lesson Occurs → Student Confirms → Payment Released to Coach
                                                      ↓
                                              48-Hour Refund Window
```

### Key Features

| Feature | Description | Benefit |
|---------|-------------|---------|
| **Escrow Hold** | Funds captured but not transferred until confirmation | Students protected from no-shows |
| **Pay-Per-Lesson** | No bulk package requirements | Reduced financial risk |
| **48-Hour Guarantee** | Full refund available within 48 hours | Quality assurance |
| **Rating-Locked Payouts** | Coaches below 4.0 rating have payouts reviewed | Maintains quality standards |
| **Dispute Resolution** | Platform mediates conflicts | Fair outcomes for both parties |

### Financial Impact

- **Reduced chargebacks:** Estimated 80% reduction vs. direct payments
- **Increased booking confidence:** Projected 25% higher conversion rate
- **Coach retention:** Lower commission justified by payment security
