# Infrastructure and Hosting Costs Research

**Source**: Financial Models Lab - Online Services Marketplace Operating Costs Analysis  
**Date**: February 2026

## Industry Benchmark: Online Services Marketplace Monthly Operating Costs

**Total Monthly Operating Budget**: $57,267 (excluding variable transaction costs)

### Fixed Cost Breakdown:

| Category | Monthly Cost | % of Total | Notes |
|----------|-------------|------------|-------|
| **Payroll** | $38,334 | 67.4% | Core team (CEO, CTO, Engineers) - largest fixed expense |
| **User Acquisition** | $10,833 | 19.0% | Buyer CAC $100, Seller CAC $250 |
| **Office Overhead** | $3,400 | 6.0% | Rent ($3,000) + utilities/internet ($400) |
| **Software/CRM** | $1,500 | 2.6% | CRM/Project Management ($800) + Marketing software ($700) |
| **Legal/Accounting** | $1,500 | 2.6% | Monthly retainer for compliance and reporting |
| **Platform Hosting (Fixed)** | $1,200 | 2.1% | Base infrastructure maintenance |
| **TOTAL FIXED** | $56,767 | 100% | Minimum monthly burn rate |

### Variable Costs (Transaction-Based):

| Category | Cost Structure | Notes |
|----------|---------------|-------|
| **Payment Processing** | 30% of GMV | Stripe/payment gateway fees (COGS) |
| **Transactional Hosting** | 15% of GMV | Server costs scale with transaction volume |

## Key Insights for BooGMe:

### 1. Payroll is the Dominant Cost (67%)
- Industry standard shows **$38K+/month** for minimal team (CEO, CTO, 2-3 engineers)
- This is your **baseline burn rate** regardless of transaction volume
- **BooGMe advantage**: Solo founder can defer salary initially, reducing this to $0-10K/month in Year 1

### 2. Platform Hosting is Surprisingly Low
- **Fixed hosting**: $1,200/month for base infrastructure
- **Variable hosting**: 15% of GMV (scales with transactions)
- **Implication**: At $100K GMV/month, hosting = $1,200 + $15,000 = $16,200 total
- **Implication**: At $500K GMV/month, hosting = $1,200 + $75,000 = $76,200 total

### 3. Payment Processing is the Real Variable Cost
- **30% of GMV** goes to payment processing fees
- This is **NOT** your commission—this is what Stripe/payment gateways charge
- **Critical**: If BooGMe charges 15% commission, and Stripe takes 2.9% + $0.30, the net is ~12% after payment fees

### 4. Customer Acquisition Costs
- Industry benchmark: **$100 per buyer, $250 per seller**
- Monthly budget: **$10,833** to acquire users
- **BooGMe advantage**: Leverage C-Squared audience (100K+ followers) for organic coach acquisition

### 5. Break-Even Timeline
- Industry model shows **12-month break-even** (December 2026 from January 2026 launch)
- Requires **$413,000 cash buffer** to cover operating deficits until breakeven
- **Critical**: This assumes aggressive growth and transaction volume

## Cost Optimization Opportunities for BooGMe:

### Eliminate Office Overhead ($3,400/month)
- Go fully remote = **$40,800/year savings**

### Reduce Initial Payroll ($38,334/month)
- Solo founder + 1 part-time developer = **$5,000-10,000/month** vs $38K
- **Year 1 savings**: ~$340,000

### Leverage Existing Audience for CAC Reduction
- C-Squared audience = organic coach acquisition
- Target CAC: $50 per coach (vs $250 industry standard)
- **Potential savings**: $200/coach × 30 coaches = $6,000/month

### Use Manus Platform for Hosting
- Manus hosting included in platform
- **Savings**: $1,200/month fixed + variable hosting costs
- **Year 1 savings**: ~$14,400 + variable costs

## Critical Takeaway:

**Industry benchmark**: $57K/month = $684K/year operating costs  
**BooGMe lean model**: $9K/month = $108K/year operating costs  
**Savings**: $576K in Year 1 by staying lean and leveraging existing assets

**Break-even calculation**:
- At $9K/month fixed costs, need $9K/month in net revenue to break even
- At 12% net margin (after payment fees), need $75K GMV/month
- **30 coaches × $2,500 GMV/month each = $75K GMV** → Break-even in Month 3-6

This is **dramatically faster** than the industry 12-month timeline because of the lean cost structure.
