# Coach Application Process Research

## Key Findings from Competitor Analysis

### Wyzant Application Process

**Timeline**: 24-48 hours for review and approval

**Requirements**:
- Must be 18+ years old
- Must reside in United States
- Must have valid Social Security Number
- No certification required

**Process**:
1. Complete online application
2. Staff review within 24-48 hours
3. Create free professional profile (pictures, videos, reviews)
4. Set own hourly rate
5. Start receiving job notifications

**Value Proposition Messaging**:
- "Millions of people search online for private tutors every day"
- "Tutors have earned more than $64,000,000"
- "Top tutors have earned $50,000+ a year"
- "Grow your tutoring business" - positioning as business building tool
- "Wyzant does all the work for you" - eliminates tedious client acquisition
- Payment protection and hassle-free billing

**Profile Components**:
- Pictures and videos
- Qualifications and experience showcase
- Student reviews and ratings
- Lesson history tracking
- Calendar integration

---

### Preply Application Process

**Timeline**: 5 business days for approval, can start teaching in as little as 3 days

**Process**:
1. Sign up to create tutor profile
2. Get approved by team (5 business days)
3. Start earning

**Value Proposition Messaging**:
- "Make a living by teaching the largest community of learners worldwide"
- "Set your own rate" ($15-25/hour average for English)
- "Teach anytime, anywhere" - no minimum commitment
- "Be your own boss"
- "Grow professionally"
- "Teach students from over 180 countries"
- "800,000+ students globally"

**Platform Benefits Highlighted**:
- Steady stream of new students
- Smart calendar
- Interactive classroom
- Convenient payment methods
- Professional development webinars
- Supportive tutor community

## Application Best Practices for BooGMe

### 1. **Optimize for Conversion (8-10 minute target)**

**Structure**:
- Multi-step progress indicator (reduces abandonment)
- Save and resume functionality
- Clear time estimate upfront ("8-10 minutes")
- Value proposition reinforcement at each step

### 2. **Essential Information to Collect**

**Step 1: Coach Profile Basics (2 min)**
- Full name
- Email
- Location/timezone
- Chess title (GM, IM, FM, NM, Expert, etc.)
- Current rating (FIDE/USCF)
- Years of coaching experience
- Profile photo upload

**Step 2: Qualifications & Expertise (2-3 min)**
- Teaching certifications (if any)
- Notable achievements (tournaments, students' successes)
- Specializations (select multiple):
  - Opening preparation
  - Middlegame strategy
  - Endgame technique
  - Tactical training
  - Tournament preparation
  - Psychological coaching
  - Junior development
  - Beginner foundations
  - Women's chess
- Target student rating range
- Teaching style/philosophy (short text)

**Step 3: Availability & Pricing (1-2 min)**
- Preferred hourly rate (with market guidance: "$45-120 based on title")
- Weekly availability (days + time slots)
- Lesson formats offered (1-on-1, group, online, in-person)
- Languages spoken

**Step 4: Teaching Approach (2-3 min)**
- Brief bio (150-300 words) - with examples/prompts
- Why you want to join BooGMe
- Sample lesson description
- Video introduction (optional but recommended)

**Step 5: Verification & Agreement (1 min)**
- Background check consent
- Platform terms agreement
- Payment setup (can be deferred to post-approval)

### 3. **Value Proposition Messaging Throughout**

**Opening Screen**:
- "Join the premier chess coaching marketplace"
- "Average coach earns $2,500-6,000/month"
- "We handle marketing, payments, and student matching - you focus on teaching"

**Progress Reinforcement** (between steps):
- Step 1 → 2: "Great start! Next, let's showcase your expertise"
- Step 2 → 3: "Your qualifications look impressive! Now let's set your availability"
- Step 3 → 4: "Almost done! Help students understand your teaching style"
- Step 4 → 5: "Final step! Review and submit"

**Value Props to Emphasize**:
1. **Student Acquisition**: "We bring students to you through AI-powered matching"
2. **Payment Protection**: "Escrow system ensures you get paid for every lesson"
3. **Business Tools**: "Calendar, scheduling, and student management included"
4. **Professional Growth**: "Analytics dashboard tracks your student retention and progress"
5. **Community**: "Join 200+ elite coaches building successful teaching businesses"

### 4. **Quality Signals to Collect**

**Indicators of Serious Coaches**:
- Detailed bio with specific teaching philosophy
- Clear specialization focus (not "everything")
- Realistic pricing (not suspiciously low)
- Professional photo
- Video introduction (strong signal)
- Specific student success stories

**Red Flags to Screen**:
- Generic/copy-pasted bios
- No clear qualifications
- Unrealistic claims
- Poor communication in application

### 5. **Post-Submission Experience**

**Immediate Confirmation**:
- "Application received! We'll review within 24-48 hours"
- What to expect next
- How to prepare (set up calendar, prepare lesson materials)

**Approval Email**:
- Welcome to BooGMe
- Next steps: Complete profile, set availability, upload video intro
- Onboarding resources (how to get your first student, pricing tips, best practices)
- Invitation to coach community (Slack/Discord)

**Rejection Email** (if applicable):
- Thank you for interest
- Specific feedback on why (if appropriate)
- Invitation to reapply after gaining more experience

## Recommended Application Flow for BooGMe

### Multi-Step Application (5 Steps, 8-10 minutes)

**Pre-Application Landing**:
- Hero: "Turn Your Chess Expertise Into a Thriving Coaching Business"
- Stats: "Join 200+ coaches earning $2,500-6,000/month"
- Benefits grid: Payment protection, AI matching, business tools, community
- Time estimate: "8-10 minutes to complete"
- CTA: "Start Application"

**Step 1: About You** (2 min)
- Personal info + chess credentials
- Progress: "1 of 5"
- Sidebar value prop: "Coaches with complete profiles get 3x more students"

**Step 2: Your Expertise** (2-3 min)
- Qualifications + specializations
- Progress: "2 of 5"
- Sidebar value prop: "Students search by specialization - be specific!"

**Step 3: Availability & Pricing** (1-2 min)
- Schedule + hourly rate
- Progress: "3 of 5"
- Sidebar value prop: "Flexible coaches earn 40% more"

**Step 4: Teaching Approach** (2-3 min)
- Bio + video intro
- Progress: "4 of 5"
- Sidebar value prop: "Coaches with video intros convert 5x better"

**Step 5: Review & Submit** (1 min)
- Summary of application
- Terms agreement
- Progress: "5 of 5"
- CTA: "Submit Application"

**Success Screen**:
- "Application Submitted!"
- "We'll review your application within 24-48 hours"
- "What happens next" timeline
- CTA: "Return to Homepage"

## Key Takeaways

1. **Keep it simple**: Wyzant and Preply both have streamlined applications focused on essential info
2. **Fast turnaround**: 24-48 hours (Wyzant) to 5 days (Preply) - set clear expectations
3. **Value prop heavy**: Both platforms emphasize earnings potential, flexibility, and business growth
4. **Low barrier to entry**: No certification required, just expertise and passion
5. **Profile quality matters**: Both emphasize professional profiles with photos, videos, and detailed bios
6. **Payment protection**: Key selling point for coaches worried about billing issues
7. **Community and support**: Professional development and peer community are important benefits
