# Pay-Per-View & Content Wall Models Research

## OnlyFans Model Economics

### Revenue Split
- **Platform fee**: 20% (creators keep 80%)
- **Revenue sources**: Subscriptions + PPV + Tips + Custom requests

### Key Insight: PPV Drives Most Revenue
**Creators with $7.99 subscription make 5-10x that amount through PPV alone**

Example: $7.99/month sub × 100 subscribers = $799/month
But PPV revenue from same 100 subscribers = $4,000-8,000/month

### Subscription Pricing (Base Access)
- **Beginner creators**: $7.99-$9.99/month
- **Established creators**: $12.99-$14.99/month  
- **Premium/Celebrity**: $24.99+/month

Lower subscription = more subscribers, but real money comes from upsells

### Pay-Per-View Pricing Ranges

#### Photo Content
- Teasing/non-nude: $3-$5
- Explicit/nude: $5-$15
- Large sets (10+ images): $10-$25

#### Video Content
- 30 sec - 1 min: $5-$15
- 5-10 min: $10-$25
- 10-20 min: $15-$50
- 20+ min: $25-$75+

#### Custom Content
- Basic custom (3-5 min): $40-$75
- Mid-tier (5-8 min, props): $80-$150
- Premium (roleplay, scripts): $150-$300+

#### Live Interactions
- 10-15 min chat: $15-$50
- 30-60 min stream: $50-$150

### PPV Strategy Tactics
1. **Tiered pricing**: Different prices for different fan segments (casual vs "whales")
2. **Bundle deals**: Package multiple items at reduced total price
3. **Limited-time offers**: Flash sales create urgency (24-48 hours)
4. **Fan segmentation**: Identify top spenders, send premium offers

### Free Page Model
- **Revenue**: 100% from PPV, tips, and upsells (no subscription)
- **Works best when**: High volume (1,000+ subscribers), consistent traffic, strong chat/sales skills
- **Conversion challenge**: Need higher PPV purchase rates to compensate for $0 subscription

## Content Paywall Best Practices

### Paywall Types
1. **Hard paywall**: All content behind payment (OnlyFans default)
2. **Metered paywall**: X free items per month, then pay (news sites)
3. **Freemium**: Basic free, premium paid (Substack model)
4. **Hybrid**: Free teasers + paid full content (most effective for creators)

### Conversion Optimization
- **Preview/teaser content**: Show value before asking for payment
- **Urgency**: Limited-time access, countdown timers
- **Social proof**: "X people unlocked this", testimonials
- **Price anchoring**: Show higher-priced option first to make target price seem reasonable
- **Bundling**: Multiple items together increase perceived value

### Typical Conversion Rates
- **Free to PPV purchase**: 5-15% of subscribers (OnlyFans)
- **Free page to paying customer**: 1-3% (content platforms)
- **Subscription to upsell**: 20-40% (established audience)

## Application to Chess Coaching

### Direct Translation Challenges
- Chess coaching is **service-based** (time commitment) vs **content-based** (one-to-many)
- Coaching requires **live interaction** vs **pre-recorded content**
- Value is in **personalized feedback** vs **consumable media**

### Potential Hybrid Models
1. **Base subscription** ($20-50/month): Access to coach's content library (game analysis videos, opening prep, puzzles)
2. **PPV content**: Premium analysis ($10-30), tournament prep courses ($50-150)
3. **Live lessons**: Separate pricing ($50-200/hour) on top of subscription
4. **Custom analysis**: Pay-per-game review ($25-75)

### Chess-Specific Content Wall Ideas
- **Free tier**: Coach profile, sample analysis, testimonials
- **Subscriber tier** ($30/month): Weekly group lessons, content library, community access
- **PPV unlocks**: Specific opening courses, GM game breakdowns, tournament prep
- **Premium tier** ($100+/month): Monthly 1-on-1 session included + all content
