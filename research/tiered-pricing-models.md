## Typical Tier Structures

### 3-Tier Model (Most Common)
1. **Basic/Supporter** ($3-10/month)
   - Early access to content
   - Behind-the-scenes updates
   - Community access
   
2. **Premium/Fan** ($10-25/month)
   - Everything in Basic
   - Exclusive content
   - Direct interaction (Q&A, polls)
   - Discounts on merchandise/services
   
3. **VIP/Patron** ($25-100+/month)
   - Everything in Premium
   - 1-on-1 access (calls, coaching sessions)
   - Custom content/personalized services
   - Recognition/credits

### 5-Tier Model (Advanced)
Some creators add intermediate tiers or ultra-premium tiers ($100-500+) for superfans

## Freemium to Premium Conversion Rates

### Industry Benchmarks
- **SaaS average**: 2-5% conversion rate from free to paid
- **High-performing SaaS**: 5-10% conversion rate
- **Content platforms**: 1-3% typical (lower due to free content abundance)

### Conversion Factors
1. **Value demonstration**: Clear differentiation between free and paid tiers
2. **Friction reduction**: Easy upgrade path, trial periods
3. **Feature gating**: Strategic limitation of free tier to create upgrade motivation
4. **Social proof**: Reviews, testimonials, member counts
5. **Urgency**: Limited-time offers, founding member pricing

## Coaching/Education Platform Tier Examples

### Typical Student Tiers
1. **Free/Browse**: View coach profiles, read reviews
2. **Single Session** ($50-200): Pay-per-lesson, no commitment
3. **Package** ($400-1500): 5-10 lesson bundle with discount
4. **Subscription** ($200-500/month): Unlimited access or monthly hours

### Typical Coach Tiers
1. **Free Listing**: Profile on platform, pay commission per booking
2. **Basic** ($0-50/month): Lower commission rate, basic analytics
3. **Pro** ($50-150/month): Lowest commission, priority placement, advanced tools
4. **Enterprise** (Custom): White-label, API access, dedicated support
